FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We have answers. If you can't find what you're looking for, contact us anytime.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes! We offer a 3-day free trial with no credit card required. You can attend up to 3 classes and experience our full platform before making any commitment.

What age groups do you teach?

We teach students of all ages — from young children (4+) to adults. Our teachers are specially trained to work with different age groups, and we have dedicated programs for kids, teenagers, and adults.

How are the classes conducted?

All classes are conducted live via our integrated video platform (or Zoom/Skype). Classes are one-on-one or in small groups. Sessions are typically 30-60 minutes depending on your plan.

Are all teachers certified?

Absolutely! All our teachers hold Ijazah certificates (chain of transmission to the Prophet ﷺ), have years of teaching experience, and undergo a rigorous selection process before joining Al-Noor Academy.

Can I choose my class schedule?

Yes! We offer flexible scheduling 7 days a week from early morning to late night. You can choose times that suit your timezone and daily routine. You can also reschedule classes when needed.

What if I want to change my teacher?

You can request a teacher change at any time, no questions asked! We want you to be comfortable with your teacher. Simply contact our support team and we'll arrange a new teacher within 24 hours.

Is the platform safe for children?

Yes, child safety is our top priority. All our teachers undergo background checks, all classes can be monitored by parents in real-time, and we have strict safety protocols in place. Parents receive reports after every class.

Do you offer family discounts?

Yes! Our Family Package covers 2 children at a discounted rate, and our Premium Plan covers up to 4 children. We also offer annual subscription discounts of up to 20%. Contact us for custom family packages.
